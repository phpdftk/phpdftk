ListRenderer.php : 21

Renders {@see ListBlock} content into a {@see ContentStream}.

The renderer is stateless; pagination logic lives at the call site (Pdf::addList measures each item and starts a new page when one would overflow). Items are independent — measure and draw one at a time, threading a running item number for numbered lists.

Coordinates: $y is the top of the area available to the item; the renderer draws downward and returns the height consumed.
